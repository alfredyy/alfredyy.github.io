A to-do list mobile app

Aug 2022

COMpanion is a gamified to-do list app made by my friend and I in a span of 3 months as part of Orbital, a self-directed software engineering programme. It was my first dive at trying to create a mobile app, something that I had not done before.

This project involved a lot of trial and error, before I could get used to coding in Javascript. I also learned a lot on the use of ReactNative and UI libraries such as React Native Paper.

Inital Phase of COMpanion

The project started out as a normal to-do list app, where users will be able to collect companions, as sort of an encouragement, and give users a mission to unlock all companions as part of their collection.

In the first week of development, we started work on using Figma, a tool to help us brainstorm and design what we wanted the app to look like. After a few iterations of messing around with the color schemes and searching the web for inspiration, we decided on a red-white color palette. White gave the app a clean modern look, and red gave it just the right dash of color, to make the design pop.

One thing I noticed about current design trends were rounded edges and minimalist designs, and that was what I wanted to focus on.

First Peer Review and Changes

As part of Orbital requirements, peer review was a necessity. It allowed us to look at our projects from a different perspective, and think of how we can improve our app.

One of the biggest criticisms we received was the lack of purpose in collecting companions. Companions are cute, but they do nothing much. This was a very valid comment that we decided to work on for the next phase of the project.

And so back to the drawing board we go! After many hours of brainstorming and idea generation, we ultimately decided on a Tamagotchi inspired game, where users could feed and entertain their companions. I didn't own a Tamagotchi growing up, but I am still a big fan of simulation games.

Final Set of Features

After a painstaking but fulfilling 3 months, we managed to complete several key features:

  • User Login and Account Creation
  • Forget Password Function
  • Home Page
  • To-do list
  • Shop
  • User Profile and Settings
